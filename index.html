Instructions

In effort to encourage more people to listen to women, I present to you Susan Says. Press the Black Go Button and listen as Susan flashes a sequence of colors. Repeat the pattern by clicking the color buttons. If you are correct, press the Next Level Button. If you are wrong, you lose the game. Remember what Susan told you!
