Susan Says

a memory game

Instructions

In effort to encourage more people to listen to women, I present to you Susan Says. Press the Black Go Button in the center and listen as Susan flashes a sequence of colors. Repeat the color pattern, by clicking the color buttons in the same order as Susan's Sequence. If your sequence matches, press the Next Level Button and the sequence will be longer. If your sequence does not match Susan's, you lose the game and the Next Level Button will be disabled. Score points by getting the sequence correct. Remember what Susan told you!
